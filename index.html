Quick Activation Overview

While each streaming service has slightly different interfaces, the general activation process follows these universal steps:

  1. Download the streaming app from your device's official app store.
  2. Open the app and look for "Sign In" or "Activate Device."
  3. Note the activation code displayed on your TV screen.
  4. Visit the activation URL shown on-screen using a phone or computer.
  5. Enter the code, sign in to your account, and start streaming.
