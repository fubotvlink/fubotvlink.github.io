Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

StreamActivate Guide ("we," "us," or "our") operates this informational website. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our site. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Collect

This is a static informational website. We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment details. We do not operate user accounts, login systems, registration forms, or e-commerce functionality.

If we use analytics services (such as privacy-focused analytics tools), we may collect anonymized, aggregate data including page views, general geographic region, browser type, device type, and referring URLs. This data cannot personally identify you.

3. Cookies

This website does not set first-party tracking cookies. If third-party services are embedded (such as analytics), they may use cookies according to their own privacy policies. You can control cookie settings through your web browser's preferences.

4. Third-Party Links

Our pages may link to external websites, including official streaming service websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of external websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any site you visit.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, the risk of data breach affecting our visitors is minimal. Our website is served over HTTPS to ensure secure communication between your browser and our server.

6. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete data. Since we do not collect personal data, these rights are generally not applicable to our website. If you have concerns, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates the most recent revision. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please refer to the contact channels described on our About Us page.
